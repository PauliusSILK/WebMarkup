Jetties in Haarlem is a cozy and intimate café, making it an ideal spot for a relaxed date. Situated conveniently near the train station, it’s perfect for couples looking to enjoy some quality time over a delicious meal or coffee before exploring the city. The café's warm, inviting atmosphere, with rustic décor and comfortable seating, sets the stage for a laid-back dining experience.

Jetties focuses on fresh, locally sourced ingredients, offering a menu that includes healthy salads, sandwiches, and delightful sugar-free desserts. Whether it's a quick coffee date or a leisurely lunch, Jetties provides the perfect ambiance for a memorable time together. Its convenient location near public transport makes it easy to find, while the emphasis on sustainability adds to the charm, making it a thoughtful choice for a romantic outing.

By Lima in Haarlem is a cozy and inviting café, perfect for a relaxed date with its warm and welcoming atmosphere. Located centrally, it offers an ideal spot to enjoy a wholesome meal, coffee, or a sweet treat made from organic, locally sourced ingredients. The café focuses on health-conscious options, including gluten-free and vegan dishes, making it an excellent choice for couples looking to share a mindful dining experience.

The charming décor and thoughtful menu create an intimate space where couples can enjoy anything from a leisurely breakfast to a late afternoon snack, ensuring a memorable date in the heart of Haarlem.
