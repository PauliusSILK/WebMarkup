Westhoff Haarlem is an excellent café for studying or remote work, offering a dedicated workspace in the back of the café. With a quiet atmosphere and comfortable seating, it's ideal for those looking for a productive environment. The café provides unlimited coffee and tea with affordable hourly or daily rates, as well as free Wi-Fi and printer access.

Its central location near the train station makes it a convenient spot for freelancers or students. For group meetings, Westhoff also offers private rooms with presentation equipment for rent.

The café at the Noord-Hollands Archief in Haarlem is an ideal spot for those looking for a peaceful place to work or study. Set within a historic setting, it offers a quiet and calming atmosphere, perfect for focusing on tasks. With free Wi-Fi and spacious seating, it’s designed to accommodate individuals seeking a productive environment.

In addition to its serene surroundings, the café serves fresh coffee, sandwiches, and light snacks, helping visitors stay energized. Its central location makes it a convenient option for those looking to work in a relaxed setting.
