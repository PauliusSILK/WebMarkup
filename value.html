Brownies & downieS Haarlem is a cozy café with a unique concept, combining great food with a social mission. It offers high-quality, affordable meals, coffee, and their signature brownies in a warm, inclusive atmosphere. The café stands out by employing people with intellectual disabilities, promoting inclusion and positivity.

Visitors to Haarlem will find it a great spot to relax, enjoy value-for-money meals, and support a meaningful cause. The menu features freshly prepared sandwiches, salads, and baked treats, perfect for a quick stop or a leisurely lunch.

In addition to its delicious offerings, the café's heartfelt mission adds to its charm, making it a rewarding experience for tourists seeking both good food and a feel-good factor. Located in the heart of Haarlem, it's easily accessible and a wonderful place to recharge during sightseeing.

Coffee Habits in Haarlem is a specialty café known for its expertly crafted coffee and relaxed vibe, perfect for visitors looking for a quiet escape from the city’s bustle. Offering high-quality brews made with ethically sourced beans from Manhattan Coffee Roasters, the café also features a selection of homemade pastries like cinnamon buns and vegan banana bread.

With two central locations, Coffee Habits caters to tourists and locals alike, providing a value-for-money experience with cozy seating and personalized service. They also offer barista workshops for coffee enthusiasts wanting to deepen their knowledge.

Ax Specialty Coffee is Haarlem’s smallest coffee bar, offering a cozy and personalized experience for visitors. Despite its small size, the café delivers big on quality, serving ethically sourced beans from local roasters like Stooker Specialty Coffee. Coffee lovers can enjoy meticulously crafted espresso drinks, complemented by a selection of freshly baked pastries and teas.

Its intimate setting and dedication to high-quality, sustainable coffee make it a hidden gem for tourists looking for a unique, value-for-money coffee experience in Haarlem’s vibrant city center.
